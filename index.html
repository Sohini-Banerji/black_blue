About Our Company

Black & Blue under­stand the need of our clients and pro­vide the best possible solution within budget. We believe in proper use of the ser­vice employees and bringing best value for money to our clients. We do not outsource our services. It gives us the liberty to propose best cost to our clients and provide a uniform service standard.

We use best quality machineries, tools and chemicals to reduce noise pollution, minimize the use of resources and protect client's property.
